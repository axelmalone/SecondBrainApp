Q2 Strategy

The wedge is the demand gate: 5 power users, 3 say they'd pay. Everything before that is just building the demo.

Local-first is the moat. Features are the demo — they're copyable. What isn't copyable is that your whole brain lives on your own disk and never leaves. Link this to #positioning and #moat.

Next milestone is TTS so the brain can talk back while you walk. After that, persistent disk-backed grounding so the index survives a relaunch instead of rebuilding every cold start.

What pricing models fit a local-first app where I can't meter usage?
Lean on value tiers, not usage. Free local tier, then a flat subscription or one-time license that unlocks sync, mobile, and voice. Per-token billing fights the local-first promise and you can't measure it anyway.

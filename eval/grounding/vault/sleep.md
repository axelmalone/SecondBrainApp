# Better Sleep

Light is the master clock. Get bright sunlight within an hour of waking and dim
the lights in the evening so your circadian rhythm knows when to wind down.

Keep the bedroom cool and completely dark, and put screens away well before bed
— the blue light tricks your brain into thinking it is still daytime.

If you cannot drift off after twenty minutes, get up and do something calm in
low light rather than lying there frustrated. A consistent wake time matters
more than a consistent bedtime.

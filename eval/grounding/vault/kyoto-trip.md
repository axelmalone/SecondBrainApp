# Kyoto Trip

Late November is peak autumn foliage, when the maple trees around the old
temples turn brilliant red and gold. Go early in the morning to beat the crowds
at the most famous gardens.

Buy a prepaid transit card for the buses, which reach the hillside shrines more
easily than the subway. A single day is enough for the eastern temple district
if you start at dawn.

Eat the multi-course kaiseki dinner at least once — it is seasonal cooking
treated as an art form.

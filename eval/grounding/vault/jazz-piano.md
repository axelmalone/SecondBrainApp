# Jazz Piano

The two-five-one is the gravitational center of jazz harmony. In C that is Dm7
to G7 to Cmaj7, and once your ear locks onto that pull you will hear it
everywhere.

Drop the root from your left hand and let the bass player cover it; voice the
third and seventh instead, since those are the notes that define the chord's
color and create smooth voice leading from one chord to the next.

Practice the progression through all twelve keys around the circle of fifths
until it lives in your fingers.
